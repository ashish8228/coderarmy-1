Player Profile

  • Virat Kohli, born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, is one of the most prolific batsmen in modern cricket.
  • Known for his aggressive and consistent batting, he has scored over 13,000 runs in One Day Internationals (ODIs) with an average of 58.18.
  • He made his international debut in 2008 and quickly rose to prominence.
  • Kohli is renowned for his ability to chase down targets, earning him the nickname "Chase Master.
  • He has captained the Indian cricket team in all formats and led them to several victories, including the 2011 Cricket World Cup and the 2017 Champions Trophy.
  • Kohli is married to Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma, and they have a daughter named Vamika.
  • He plays for Royal Challengers Bangalore in the Indian Premier League (IPL) and Delhi in domestic cricket.
  • Kohli retired from T20I cricket in 2024 after India's T20 World Cup win.
  • He is also known for his dedication to fitness, which has set a new standard in Indian cricket.
